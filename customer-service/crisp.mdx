ShipAny integrates with [Crisp](https://crisp.chat/) customer service system. With simple configuration, you can integrate the web chat widget into your website.

## Quick Start

Follow the steps below to quickly integrate the Crisp chat widget into your website.

<Steps>
<Step>Create a Crisp Account</Step>

Visit the [Crisp website](https://crisp.chat/) and register an account.

<Step>Add Your Website</Step>

Go to the [Crisp dashboard](https://app.crisp.chat/) and add the domain of the website where you want to integrate the chat widget.

Navigate to `Settings -> Workspace Settings -> Setup & Integrations` page and copy the `Website ID`.

![](https://cdn.shipany.ai/shipany/imgs/docs/crisp-settings.png)

<Step>Configure Crisp Chat Widget</Step>

In your project admin panel, go to `Settings -> Customer Service -> Crisp` panel, and enter the `Website ID` you copied in the previous step into the `Crisp Website ID` field.

Enable the `Crisp Enabled` toggle and click the `Save` button to save the configuration.

![](https://cdn.shipany.ai/shipany/imgs/docs/crisp-admin-settings.png)

<Step>Test the Chat Widget</Step>

On your project homepage, refresh the page. You should see a customer service icon in the bottom right corner. Click it to open the Crisp chat widget.

![](https://cdn.shipany.ai/shipany/imgs/docs/crisp-chatbox.png)

Users can send messages through the chat widget on your website.

<Step>Manage Customer Messages</Step>

In the Crisp dashboard, go to the `Inbox` page to view and reply to user messages.

![](https://cdn.shipany.ai/shipany/imgs/docs/crisp-message.png)

</Steps>
